1. General Information

The "Craftviser" app ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our app for managing activities and goals.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address - for registration, login, and account recovery

  • Profile information - avatar, biography, and profile settings

2.2 App Data

  • Activities - names, descriptions, categories, and tags of activities
  • Goals - user-defined goals and progress tracking
  • Saved activities - activities saved by the user
  • Tried activities - activities marked as tried by the user
  • Activity folders - user-created collections of activities
  • Friends and connections - relationships between users
  • User settings - preferences and configurations

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address - for security and abuse prevention
  • Device information - device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage logs - for troubleshooting and app improvement

3. How We Use Data

  • Service provision - to operate the app's core functions
  • Authentication - for secure login and access

  • Personalization - to customize content based on user preferences
  • App improvement - to analyze usage and optimize performance
  • Technical support - to resolve user issues
  • Security - to protect against fraud and abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase - a reliable cloud platform for data storage. All data:

  • Is stored in secure data centers
  • Is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Is protected from unauthorized access
  • Is regularly backed up

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent
  • When necessary for service provision (e.g., hosting through Supabase)

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access - obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification - correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure - delete your data
  • Right to restriction - limit data processing
  • Right to portability - receive data in a structured format

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary for:

  • Providing app services
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Resolving disputes
  • Ensuring security

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
